Marketing Manager - New York, NY

Job Description:

We are looking for a creative and dynamic Marketing Manager to lead and execute our marketing strategies. The ideal candidate will be responsible for developing and managing marketing campaigns, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and analyzing market trends to enhance brand awareness and drive business growth. This is a full-time position based in New York with a competitive salary and benefits.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and implement marketing strategies that drive brand growth and customer engagement.
  • Collaborate with sales and product teams to align marketing efforts with business objectives.
  • Analyze market trends, consumer behavior, and competitors to optimize marketing campaigns.
  • Manage marketing budgets and ensure cost-effective strategies.
  • Lead and mentor a team of marketing professionals, providing guidance and support.

Requirements:

  • BS in Marketing, Business, or related field (or equivalent experience).
  • 5+ years of experience in marketing, with at least 2 years in a managerial role.
  • Strong understanding of digital marketing strategies, social media platforms, and SEO/SEM.
  • Excellent communication and leadership skills.
  • Ability to analyze data and generate actionable insights for campaign improvement.
  • Experience with marketing automation tools and CRM software is a plus.

Pay Information:

This is a full-time position offering a competitive salary, with a range of $90,000 - $120,000 per year depending on experience. In addition to salary, the position offers comprehensive benefits including health insurance, retirement plans, and paid time off.
