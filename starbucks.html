Starbucks Barista Job

Starbucks Barista - Duluth, GA

Job Description:

Join the Starbucks team as a Barista, where you’ll create delicious beverages and provide excellent customer service. As a Barista, you’ll have the chance to interact with customers, make specialty coffee drinks, and ensure the store runs smoothly during each shift. This is a part-time position offering a competitive hourly wage of $10/hour.

Responsibilities:

  • Prepare and serve coffee and other beverages.
  • Provide exceptional customer service, addressing customer needs and questions.
  • Ensure a clean and organized work environment.
  • Manage cash register and handle payments.
  • Follow safety and cleanliness procedures.

Requirements:

  • Must be at least 18 years old.
  • Previous barista or customer service experience is a plus but not required.
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment.
  • Strong communication skills and attention to detail.
  • Must be able to work flexible hours, including weekends and holidays.

Pay Information:

This is a part-time position offering a competitive wage of $10/hour. As a Barista, you will receive tips in addition to your hourly wage. Flexible shifts are available to accommodate your schedule!
